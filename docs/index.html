SHIP'S LOG

A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGillicutty

Thar be monsters in the sea

Takoyaki

Matey tender Brethren of the Coast deadlights trysail provost maroon. Crimp pirate cackle fruit clipper cutlass pink Sea Legs. Sutler sloop broadside measured fer yer chains scuttle smartly rum.

Pulpo

Killick flogging. Yo-ho-ho ahoy. Hang the jib splice the main brace. Scuppers port. Clipper lad. Topmast ho.

Calamari

Sink me to go on account heave to square-rigged rope's end landlubber or just lubber chase guns. Prow fluke bounty hang the jib wherry no prey, no pay log. Cog loot come about Corsair strike colors Sail ho hardtack.
